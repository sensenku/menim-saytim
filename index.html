About Me

We can make it together

I am Mehman Jamaland I’m a CG Artist from Azerbaijan. My work includes modeling, texturing, painting, drawing, lighting and rendering. I mostly do character and creature modeling as well as concepting. My main softwares are Blender 3D and Substance Painter, and I have worked in character development for animation and game studios, interior and exterior designer for agencies and mockup making for freelance artists.

I also find a lot of joy in creating storytelling through pictures, and this is one of the things I enjoy doing the most.
